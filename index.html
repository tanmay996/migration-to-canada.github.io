Permanent Residence

(For Professionals, Business Owners, Executives and Trades)
There are three categories under the Federal Skilled Worker Class:

Category 1: For persons whose occupation is on the current list of occupations identified in the Ministerial Instructions and have evidence of one year of continuous full-time (or full-time equivalent) experience in that occupation;

Category 2: For persons who have an arranged employment offer (AEO)with a Canadian employer; or

Category 3: For students or workers who are legally residing in Canada, and have done so for at least 12 months immediately before submitting their application.

Contact us for more information.

Other programs for skilled workers who desire to become permanent residents include Provincial Nominees and the Canadian Experience Class.

For more information on the services we offer for businesses, please see our Business Immigration section.
